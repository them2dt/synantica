core features:

- students get an overview over the upcoming events.
- students can join events, via 3rd-party-link.
- students can share an event, through links.
- student can access event-related-resources through Google-drive-links.
- students can search and filter events (by category, date, subject).
- students can view detailed event information.
- students can register/login to save preferences.
- students can switch between light/dark theme.
